Origami Designs

Origami instructions and Diagrams


Step by Step diagrams are probably the most popular and easiest way to show how to fold things out of paper. However, it can be a tedious task to mull over the hundreds of instructions online. Therefore, we have combined some of the internets greatests resources for you.

Chameleon Design

(click on the image for tutorial)

chameleon origami instructions

  • Step 1) Fold and unfold the paper in half both ways. Then fold the top and bottom to the centre.
  • Step 2) Fold and unfold both sides to the centre.
  • Step 3) Fold all the corners down along the diagonal dotted lines.
  • Step 4) Open up each corner and Squash Fold them flat. See the next step to see how each corner looks after the Squash Fold
  • Step 5) Turn the paper over.
  • Step 6) Fold the edges of the paper down along the dotted lines.
  • Step 7) Turn the paper over
  • Step 8) Fold the 4 flaps of paper out along the dotted lines.
  • Step 9) Fold the paper in half over to the back.
  • Step 10) Inside Reverse Fold the paper up along the dotted line.
  • Step 11) Outside Reverse Fold the other side of the model down along the dotted line to form the tail.
  • Step 12) Make another Outside Reverse Fold on the end of the tail.

Dragon Design

(click on the image for tutorial)

Dragon origami instructions

  • Step 1: Begin With a Square Piece of Paper.
  • Step 2: Fold the Paper in Half Diagonally.
  • Step 3: Fold This Triangle in Half Again.
  • Step 4: Open One of the Triangle Flaps.
  • Step 5: Fold All Sides of the Square to the Crease in the Middle.
  • Step 6: Open the Model, and Flatten Into a Crane Base.
  • Step 7: Create a Sink Fold.(optional)
  • Step 8: Fold the Sides Down.(do only if you do the sink fold step)
  • Step 9: Fold the Open Flaps Into an "X"
  • Step 10: Undo and Collapse.
  • Step 11: Fold the Bottom Half of the Model Out.
  • Step 12: Make an Inside Reverse Fold.
  • Step 13: Open One Flap and Collapse.
  • Step 14: Fold the Dragon's Wings Up.
  • Step 15: Fold the Head and Front Feet.
  • Step 16: Make the Back Feet.
  • Step 17: Optional: Crimp Fold the Tail.
  • Step 18: Stylize the Dragon!
